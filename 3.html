The worst case scenario is something like 1984. Some company or entity supresses the people's knowledge of cybersec and has complete control over their lives with their lack of knowledge and the people cannot rebel because they do not know that the suppressor is even doing anything.

What are the best-case scenarios? Explain using examples.

The best case scenario is already being enacted. It is about people being educated about cybersecurity in school and in the workplace. In most workplaces there is a cybersecurity policy and annual or monthly cybersecurity checkup meetings. This is important because some big businesses are large targets and they can be very vulnerable if no security policy is in place. There are also many companies whose job is to try and hack into businesses to test and patch security. Police are also coming into ACT public schools to talk about cybersecurity and there are a plethora of YouTube channels educating people about cybersecurity.
